How to create Pepper & walnut hummus with veggie dippers

Time: 15 mins
Yield size: Serves 2 people

Ingredients:

  • 400g can drained chickpeas
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 (about 100g) large roasted red pepper from a jar (not in oil)
  • 1 tablespoon tahini paste
  • 1/2 teaspoonlemon juice
  • 4 walnut halves, chopped
  • 2 courgettes, cut into batons
  • 2 carrots, cut into batons
  • 2 celery sticks, cut into batons


Steps:

  1. Put the chickpeas, garlic, pepper, tahini and lemon juice in a bowl. Blitz with a hand blender or in a food processor to make a thick pur√©e. Stir in the walnuts. Pack into pots, if you like, and serve with the veggie sticks. Will keep in the fridge for two days, although the vegetables are best prepared fresh to preserve their vitamins.
